RenalRisk Pro — Clinician Evaluation

Thank you for participating in this study. Your responses will directly inform the development of an AI-powered clinical decision support system for early Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) risk prediction using Explainable AI (XAI).

Study Objective
We aim to evaluate the clinical suitability of the system's outputs and explanations through your hands-on experience with the prototype, and to assess the clarity, relevance, and trustworthiness of its XAI features.
How this survey works
  • You will enter a specific clinical case into the live prototype
  • Review the risk score and the explanations carefully
  • Answer targeted questions based on what you observed
  • Estimated time: 10–15 minutes
Confidentiality
Participation is voluntary and fully anonymous. Data will be used solely for academic research purposes as part of a Master's thesis at King Abdulaziz University, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.
